What is Homeqube?

Homeqube is a home building application powered by artificial intelligence (AI) that empowers
the user to build his future home. Through its built-in network of manufacturers, suppliers,
contractors, architects, and engineers, Homeqube helps future homeowners optimize their
design according to every budget, taste, lifestyle, and more

Our Mission

Make the home building processes accessible to all through AI and blockchain technologies,
serving individuals’ and families’ requirements for long-term sustained success.

Our Vision

Provide a better home building ecosystem through a socio-techno-economic platform that
creates more justice, equity, and liberty for the future.

Core Values

Transparency

Our actions and relationships are driven by honesty, authenticity, trust, and accountability.

Social Justice

We promote an equitable home building process through equal access to technology,
experienced partnerships, and opportunities

Love of God and Neighbor

We care deeply about those we serve, as guided by mutual respect and a heart for service.

Who We Are

Homeqube is developed by a team of experts from multiple disciplines with years of experience
in architecture, engineering, manufacturing, and construction.

Grounded on the premise that the main population of built architecture was built on top-down
construction processes, we address the limited opportunities for design and building efficiencies
and sustainability through our fresh approach. We want to introduce a new batch of potential
aesthetics for the next century, living with AI in our midst.

Our Advantage


A decentralized online platform for home design has never been done before as it comes on the
heels of global problems that the world has never experienced before: a once-in-a-lifetime
pandemic, the worsening and accelerating impacts of climate change, and heightened cyber
insecurity.

Through this application, we envision a significant reduction in common architectural and
engineering inefficiencies, such as material and labor waste, while giving both the supplier and
end user access to useful information and resources in the home building process.
